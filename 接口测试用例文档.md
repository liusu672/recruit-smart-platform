## 一、认证模块（AuthController）

| 用例编号 | 接口描述 | 请求方式 | 请求路径 | 请求参数 | 预期响应 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| AUTH-01 | 用户登录 | POST | `/api/auth/login` | `{"username":"admin","password":"123456"}` | `200 {"code":200,"data":{"token":"xxx","tokenType":"Bearer","userInfo":{...}}}` |
| AUTH-02 | 用户登录（密码错误） | POST | `/api/auth/login` | `{"username":"admin","password":"wrong"}` | `400 {"code":400,"message":"用户名或密码错误"}` |
| AUTH-03 | 用户登录（用户不存在） | POST | `/api/auth/login` | `{"username":"unknown","password":"123456"}` | `404 {"code":404,"message":"用户名或密码错误"}` |
| AUTH-04 | 获取当前用户 | GET | `/api/auth/current-user` | Header: `Authorization: Bearer {token}` | `200 {"code":200,"data":{"userId":1,"username":"admin","roleCode":"ADMIN",...}}}` |
| AUTH-05 | 候选人注册 | POST | `/api/auth/register` | `{"username":"newuser","password":"pass123","confirmPassword":"pass123","name":"张三","phone":"13800138000","gender":"男","age":25,"education":"本科"}` | `200 {"code":200,"data":{"token":"xxx","tokenType":"Bearer","userInfo":{...}}}` |
